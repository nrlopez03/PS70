# bio
    
# ---------------------------------

i lived most of my life in mumbai and pune, india. at age 16, i moved to armenia to attend an international boarding school called united world college dilijan where i lived and learned alongside 200 students from over 90 different countries. after graduating and before coming to harvard, i took a gap year and worked at a similar school in tel aviv focused on israeli-palestinian conflict resolution.

some of my personal and professional interests include early-stage investing, real estate, poverty alleviation, taxation policy, comparative politics, social democracy, linguistics, travel & cultural immersion, the outdoors, productivity hacking, human longevity, and mixology.
