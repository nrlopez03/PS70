# Final Project Idea
## Background

I enrolled in this course hoping to make something useful and practical for a final project. With a goal to design and build a device I would use, I spoke with my roommates about potential areas of opportunity. We discussed minor inconveniences and various ways in which we could implement solutions.

One opportunity, we realized, was within our own suite. We have a television in our common room and very often lose the TV remote. We came up with a potential solution for frequently losing the remote.

## Proposal

I aspire to make a tool that makes the remote easy to locate when placed. I would design a dock for the TV remote, where the remote would be placed when not in use. (The remote is also rechargeable via USB-C and could be charged in the dock.) The remote would be attached to a smaller device which would interact with the dock. When the remote, and thus the smaller device, is removed from the dock, a timer (1-3 minutes) would begin on the dock. If the timer is completed before the remote is detected as returned, the dock would sound an alarm.

Ideally, there are various radio emitters around the room, which would enable us to triangulate the location of the remote. The alarm, given the triangualted coordinates and pre-determined location names of the common room (i.e. couch, table, etc.), the alarm would provide the current location of the remote, allowing for it to be easily located and retuned.
